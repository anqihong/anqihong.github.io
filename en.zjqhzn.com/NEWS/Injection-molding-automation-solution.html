Injection molding automation solution

2020-03-02

The injection molding industry is developing rapidly, traditional manual injection molding methods are inefficient, and potential safety hazards can no longer meet the needs of society and customers. Many companies and factories are seeking breakthroughs and are gradually joining large injection molding automation teams. Aiqihhon's main industries are automated manipulators and Injection molding automation equipment. The injection molding automation solution automation solution we provide are the most comprehensive, reasonable, and hassle-free.


Because injection molding automation can greatly increase productivity and reduce production costs, it can stabilize and improve the quality of injection molded products and avoid losses caused by human error. Therefore, the role of automated injection in production has become increasingly important.


With the development of the injection molding industry, more and more automated equipment will be used for various processes, such as mold loading, mixing, unloading, and waste recycling. Plastic occupies a very important position in our industry and people's livelihood. Many materials have also been replaced by plastics; plastic molding includes: injection molding, blister molding, blow molding, extrusion molding, die casting, etc., among which injection molding is the most widely used. It is very popular in the automotive, communications, electronics, electrical, home appliances, medical, cosmetics, daily necessities and office supplies industries.


In the traditional injection molding process, from the earliest manual molding to mold molding. The molding quality is related to the performance of the injection molding machine itself, the molding process and the surrounding environment. The molding efficiency is related to the accuracy of the mold, the molding process and the output. The application of plastic molding automation is extremely common. In the production process, automation is mainly used in the following areas:

1. The manipulator takes out the in-mold product and replaces the original semi-automatic production with fully automatic production.

2. Take the product out of the mold, and then embed the product in the mold (marking, embedding metal, secondary molding, etc.).

3. Automatically pack after taking out the manipulator and enter the warehouse automatically.

4. automatic feeding system for molding materials, waste recycling system.

5. Plant-wide production control system, etc.


Because the molded products are different, the automation application is also very complicated, and it can be replaced with lower manual efficiency to ensure the process of the molded products, so it is increasingly widely used. The take-out manipulator of an injection molding machine is most widely used in molding automation.
