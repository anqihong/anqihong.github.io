    AIQIHHON SMART TECHNOLOGY

    AIQIHHON specializes in manufacturing manipulators, with a rich lineup of manipulator products, which can meet various customer needs! Manipulators that play an important role in plastic molding. We provide a series of services for the design, production and sales of non-standard manipulators.

    Pickup, Insert, Stack, Sort, Cut manipulator

    Manipulator equipment can greatly improve the production efficiency of the enterprise and avoid the impact on production caused by human factors. Reduce manual labor intensity, reduce work accidents, improve work efficiency and product quality.

    AIQIHHON's Manipulator product lineup is rich and can meet various customer needs! Mainly including Pickup, Insert, Stack, Sort, Cut manipulator.

    Learn More

    In-mould labeling manipulator

    In-mould labeling is a fully automated system, which is mainly integrated by injection molding machine, mold, label, manipulator and robot. Injection molding machine technology, robotic static electricity, robotic vacuum pump, robotic system action, standard paper substrate, standard paper static electricity, mold quality, surrounding environment, injection molding machine cooling system, raw material flow, etc. will all affect product quality. Qihong has a complete pre-sales and after-sales service system. We solve most of the labeling product quality problems for customers.

    Learn More

    Tube blank removal, in-mould cooling manipulator

    The composition of the tube blank removal manipulator is generally composed of an execution system, a drive system, a control system, and the like. The execution and driving system is mainly designed to complete the normal function of the arm. The mechanical parts are driven by pneumatic or motor to achieve the function of taking objects.

    Thin-wall pickup, high-speed manipulator

    The composition of the thin-wall pickup manipulator is generally composed of an execution system, a drive system, a control system, and the like. The execution and driving system is mainly designed to complete the normal function of the arm. The mechanical parts are driven by pneumatic or motor to achieve the function of taking objects with high-speed.

    Loading and unloading manipulator

    In the traditional manufacturing process, almost all humans are used to load and unload the processing machine tools. This original operation method has the disadvantages of high labor intensity, and difficult to balance output and quality. In the "environmental substitution" environment, more and more manufacturing companies have launched Loading and unloading manipulator, which has reduced labor costs, increased production and quality, and enhanced the competitiveness of enterprises.
