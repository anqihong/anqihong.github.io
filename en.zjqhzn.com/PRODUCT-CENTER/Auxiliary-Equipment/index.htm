    AIQIHHON SMART TECHNOLOGY

    AIQIHHON supply various industrial auxiliary equipment, including Grinder, Chiller, Mould temperature machine, Dryer, Color Mixer, Conveyor. We provide solutions for various application environments with reasonable fees, high standards and flexible products.

    Grinder

    The Grinder has the characteristics of compact design, practical model, beautiful appearance, good seal, stable performance, convenient operation, simple decoration, durable, wide range of application, and high production efficiency. It is the ideal processing equipment for processing fine materials.

    Chiller

    Chiller is widely used in laser equipment, CNC machine tools, reactors, injection molding equipment, electroplating equipment, vacuum coating, LED equipment, environmental cleaning equipment, linear motors, high-speed shafts, high-precision positioning beams, process molds and other cooling equipment Equipment and component temperature requirements

    Learn More

    Mould temperature machine

    The mould temperature machine is also called the mould temperature control machine, which is widely used in various industries such as plastic molding, light guide plate die casting, rubber tires, rollers, chemical reaction kettles, bonding, and mixing. In a broad sense, it is called temperature control equipment, which includes temperature control in both heating and cooling.

    Learn More

    Dryer

    Because most engineering plastics are mostly hygroscopic, when the plastic is taken out of the moisture-proof sealed packaging bag and exposed to the atmosphere, it starts to absorb moisture from the atmosphere. If it is a conventional traditional hot air bucket dryer, The outside air with moisture is used to dry the plastic, so it cannot prevent the plastic from continuing to absorb moisture. The industrial dryer adopts a closed circulation system, which can not be affected by the external weather. Whether it is dry or wet weather, the material can be dried well, no anti-humidity, and high product molding quality.

    Learn More

    Color Mixer

    The color mixer is a machine that uses the vortex-type high-speed rotation of the blades to make the plastic particles (or resin powder) roll and collide with friction and heat at high speed, so that the materials can be mixed while the water is quickly evaporated from the exhaust pipe to achieve the purpose of drying.

    Learn More
